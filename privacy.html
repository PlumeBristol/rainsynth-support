Privacy Policy

Overview

RainSynth is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

RainSynth collects no data from its users. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies, and no advertising.

All audio synthesis happens entirely on your device. No information is sent to us or to any third party.

Third-Party Services

RainSynth does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or data collection SDKs.

Internet Access

RainSynth does not require an internet connection to function. The app works entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Because RainSynth does not collect any data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page. Since RainSynth does not collect data, we do not anticipate any changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at support@rainsynth.app.
