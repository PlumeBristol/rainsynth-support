About

RainSynth generates rain, forest, and fire soundscapes in real time — no loops, no recordings. Shape the sound by dragging the glowing cursor across the pad, or let dynamic mode drift it slowly for hands-free ambience.

Privacy

RainSynth collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. All audio runs entirely on your device.

FAQ

  • Does it work in the background?
    Yes — audio continues when your screen is locked or you switch apps.
  • Does it work in silent mode?
    Yes — RainSynth uses the media audio route and plays through silent mode.
  • What are the three synths?
    RainSynth (rain), ForestSynth (filtered nature noise), and FireSynth (crackling fire). Swipe left or right to switch.
  • What is dynamic mode?
    Dynamic mode slowly wanders the cursor around the pad automatically, creating ever-changing sound without any interaction.
